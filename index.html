Lead Paint Detection & Safety Evaluation

Lead-based paint was widely used in Canadian homes until 1978. If your property was constructed before this date, there's a significant likelihood that lead paint exists somewhere on the premises. Our inspection services identify these hazards, assess their risk level, and provide actionable recommendations to protect your family or tenants.

We serve property owners, prospective buyers, renovation contractors, and property managers throughout Windsor, Toronto, Ottawa, and across Ontario.

01

Home Purchase Inspections

Planning to buy a home built before 1978? Our pre-purchase lead paint evaluation gives you critical information before closing.

  • Visual inspection of painted surfaces
  • XRF analyzer readings for instant results
  • Lab sample coordination when needed
  • Detailed findings report within 48 hours
02

Pre-Renovation Risk Assessment

Disturbing lead paint during renovation creates dangerous dust. Know what you're dealing with before starting work.

  • Targeted testing of renovation areas
  • Lead hazard risk classification
  • Safe work practice recommendations
  • Contractor coordination support
03

Child Safety Evaluations

Young children are especially vulnerable to lead exposure. We focus on high-risk areas where children spend time.

  • Friction surface testing (doors, windows)
  • Chewable surface evaluation
  • Deteriorating paint identification
  • Immediate hazard prioritization
